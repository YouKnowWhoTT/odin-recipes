Pasta

Description

Pasta is a type of dough made from flour, water, and sometimes eggs. It comes in countless shapes and sizes, each with its own unique texture and cooking time. Pasta is a versatile ingredient that can be paired with a variety of sauces, vegetables, and proteins.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil: Fill a large pot with water and add a generous amount of salt. Bring the water to a rolling boil.
  2. Add the pasta: Carefully add the pasta to the boiling water, stirring immediately to prevent it from sticking together.
  3. Cook according to package directions: Cook the pasta for the recommended time on the package. This time can vary depending on the type of pasta.
  4. Check for doneness: To test for doneness, remove a piece of pasta and bite it. It should be tender but still have a slight bite.
  5. Drain the pasta: When the pasta is cooked to your liking, drain it in a colander.
  6. Serve immediately: Serve the pasta hot with your favorite sauce.
