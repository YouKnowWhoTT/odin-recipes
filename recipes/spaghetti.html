Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a long, thin strand of pasta that is typically served with a sauce. It's one of the most popular pasta shapes worldwide and is versatile enough to pair with a variety of flavors.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil: Fill a large pot with water and add a generous amount of salt. Bring the water to a rolling boil.
  2. Add the spaghetti: Carefully add the spaghetti to the boiling water, making sure to stir it immediately to prevent it from sticking together.
  3. Cook according to package directions: Cook the spaghetti for the recommended time on the package. This typically ranges from 8-12 minutes, depending on the thickness of the spaghetti.
